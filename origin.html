Where I'm From

I was born and raised in beautiful San Diego, California. Growing up, I attended school here and experienced all the amazing things this city has to offer, from its stunning beaches to its vibrant culture.

San Diego is known for its perfect weather year-round, beautiful coastal views, and outdoor activities. Whether it's a day at the beach, a hike in the mountains, or exploring the lively city, there's always something to do in this wonderful city.

I'm proud to call San Diego my home and cherish all the memories made here!
